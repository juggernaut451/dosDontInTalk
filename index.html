Don’t use complete sentences in your slides

Don’t use hard-to-read fonts or garish backgrounds

Don’t use effects

Don’t use any acronyms

Don’t use a corporate slide template

Don't try to be funny if you’re not

Don't put everything on one slide

Don't use transitions, animation & sound

Don't use hard-to-read color combinations and fonts

Don't exceed the timeline !!

Do ssssssss.....

what does this audience want to hear?

start with the problem

Use story-telling and your passion.

Engage the audience during your talk

very big text sizes and images that fill the whole screen

Use a dark background

Use a spell checker

One idea per slide

Don’t Try to Cover Too Much Material.

Admit You Don’t Have All the Answers

End your presentation by showing a slide with a key question, or action point aimed at the audience, to encourage discussions afterwards.

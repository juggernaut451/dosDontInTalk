Network Load Performance

RAIL

Google Benchmark

  • R = Response
  • A = Animation
  • I = Idle
  • L = Load

Response

  • Tap/Click should paint in less than 100ms

Animation

  • Each frame completes in less than 16ms
  • Drag to paint in less than 16ms

Idle

  • Use IDLE time to proactive schedule work
  • Complete that work in 50ms

Load

  • Satisfy "RESPONSE" goals during full load
  • Get first meaning full paint in 1,000ms
